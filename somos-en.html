Who are we?

Foundations and Vision

Somos+ is founded as a movement that calls upon all Cubans who wish to participate in the beautiful and difficult adventure of helping to build a modern, prosperous and free country.

The men and women who make up this movement assume the challenge and responsibility that history imposes on us: to demonstrate that within our battered society there are still people who can raise the flag of hope and undertake, both materially and spiritually, the reconstruction of the homeland.
