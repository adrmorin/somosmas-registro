Regulations for the Presidential Elections of the Somos+ Political Movement

(Agreement C.E. / April 3, 2021)

This regulation is the result of an extensive and prolonged deliberative process carried out across all structures of the Movement, to adapt the regulations approved in 2017 to the current conditions characterized by the significant growth and rise of our Movement as well as the new geopolitical scenario. These regulations aim to guarantee a transparent process that faithfully expresses the will of our members.

  • Analysis and correction of its predecessor by the Executive Council.
  • Analysis and corrections of the new proposal in the Expanded Council of the Movement.
  • Analysis and corrections in meetings with all members.
Article 1
Every four years, the Executive Council will call for free and democratic elections to elect or re-elect the position of President of the Somos+ Political Movement, with two months' notice before the electoral process.
Article 2
All members of the Movement with at least three months of membership have the right to vote.
Article 3
An "Electoral Commission" will be formed to oversee and organize the entire process, as well as to count the votes and announce the final results.
Article 4
The Electoral Commission ensures that every candidate has the possibility and media coverage to campaign, guaranteeing equal conditions for each candidate.
Article 5
Any active member who complies with our "Ideas and Principles" can compete for the position of President if they meet the following requirements:
  • Be born in Cuba.
  • Be over 18 years old.
  • Be an example of ethical and professional authority.
  • Have no less than two years of membership in the Movement.
