Frequently Asked Questions

BACK IDEAS & PRINCIPLES ELECTION REGULATIONS
How can I join the Somos+ Movement?
Integration is done through registration on our official portal or by contacting our team directly. We encourage the creation of local "clubs" both on the island and abroad so that each member has a space for active participation.
How do you plan to change the system in Cuba?
Our strategy is based on deep educational reform and citizen empowerment. We seek a peaceful transition to democracy through the formation of universal values and conscious civil pressure, based on concrete proposals for the country.
How is the movement funded?
Somos+ is supported exclusively through voluntary contributions from its members and supporters. Maintaining our ideological sovereignty is vital, which is why we do not accept funds from foreign governments or external political foundations.
Is Somos+ a political party?
We define ourselves as a movement of political and civic vanguard. Although we aspire to participate in a plural democratic system of parties in the future, our current role is to prepare citizenship and propose a modern vision of the nation.
What is your position on education and health?
We defend plural and high-quality models. In education, we seek to eliminate state indoctrination. In health, we propose that the State guarantee basic access while allowing for the coexistence of services that dignify the work of professionals.
What role does the exile play in the movement?
The exile is a fundamental pillar. Their work is to make the Cuban reality visible to the world, mobilize international support, and serve as linguistic and moral support for those working for change from within the island.
